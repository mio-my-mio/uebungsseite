Ergebnisse

1985

William Afton goes on a murdering spree inside of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. This is later dubbed by the public and media as the "Missing Children's Incident". The bodies are stuffed inside of the newer four animatronics and the old Fredbear suit. Customers notice foul smells start to come from the suits, and parents contact police reporting blood and mucus around the eyes and mouths of the animatronics. Freddy Fazbear's Pizza shuts down.

1985-1987

The old animatronics are retro-fitted with newer technology, but scrapped and used for spare parts.

1987

The events of Five Nights at Freddy's 2 take place. Freddy Fazbear's Pizza reopens with a new location in early November, featuring the new Toy animatronics, as well as The Puppet once again. The remaining five animatronics from the old Freddy Fazbear's Pizza location were initially planned to be reused, being retrofitted with the new technology, but were inevitably stored in the back room, being used for parts instead.
